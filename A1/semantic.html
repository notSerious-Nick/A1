Campus Coffee Guide

Welcome to the Campus Coffee Guide. This site helps students find good coffee on and around campus.

On-Campus

Two Creek has a nice outdoor area to enjoy good weather. The Starbucks in the bookstore is a good place to show off your Stanley cups.

Off-Campus

Publik Eds is housed in a once iconic dive lunch spot. Cucina is a good place to go and wonder how people have so much time to linger over pricey lunches.

Hours

Most campus locations are open from 8am to 6pm. Hours may vary during finals week.
